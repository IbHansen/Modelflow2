Q-JEM Equation Documentation

This document lists all equations in the Quarterly Japanese Economic Model (Q-JEM).

In equations, deterministic variables are denoted in green font. Coefficients and their standard errors (in < >) are denoted in pink font. @movav(x, n) stands for n periods backward moving average of x, and @movsum(x, n) stands for n periods backward moving sum of x. d(x) and dlog(x) denote difference and log difference of x, respectively. D~ stands for a dummy variable. D083 takes the value of 1 at 2008/3Q, and 0 otherwise. D083Z takes the value of 1 from 2008/3Q to the end, and 0 otherwise. D083091 takes the value of 1 from 2008/3Q to 2009/1Q, and 0 otherwise.
